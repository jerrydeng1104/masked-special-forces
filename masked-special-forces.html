About Masked Special Forces

Masked Special Forces is an action-packed multiplayer first-person shooter game that puts you in the shoes of a commander on the battlefield. As a talented warrior, you'll need to work with your team, employ strategic thinking, and defeat your opponents one by one.

With hyper-realistic visuals and smooth gameplay, this game is just as captivating as iconic console titles. Access a range of upgradable weapons, an arsenal, kill messages, and victory dances from the main menu that will enhance your gaming experience.

In this fast-paced action game, you can dive straight into intense combat or thoroughly customize your gaming experience before the gunfight. Choose from available rooms or create your own by selecting from detailed maps.

Since its initial release, Masked Special Forces has been loved and played by thousands, making it the forerunner of an entire sequel series with six different FPS games. If you enjoy Masked Special Forces, we highly recommend trying Masked Forces Zombie Survival.

How to Play

You can start the game by joining a room created by other players or by creating your own. To create your own room, you'll need to name it, select the maximum number of players, game mode, loop time, bot count, and map.

Once you enter a room, the battle begins immediately. Take your position and start eliminating enemies. Learn how to aim and shoot, and explore the other controls, displays, arsenal, and maps detailed below.

Pro Tips for Masked Special Forces

Dominate the battlefield with these strategies from top players:

  • Always keep moving to avoid becoming an easy target
  • Learn the maps to understand strategic positions and hiding spots
  • Choose weapons that match your playstyle - fast shooters might prefer SMGs, while precision players might opt for sniper rifles
  • Use the slide mechanic (Q while running) to quickly take cover or evade enemy fire
  • Communicate with teammates in Team Deathmatch and Capture the Flag modes
  • Aim for headshots to maximize damage
  • Utilize grenades to flush out enemies from behind cover
  • Remember to use medkits (H key) when your health is low
  • Switch between weapons based on the range of engagement
